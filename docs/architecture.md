
# HachiClaw AI Architecture

HachiClaw AI is a behavioral intelligence system designed to detect risky trading behavior patterns.

## System Flow

User Activity  
↓  
Behavioral Engine  
↓  
Behavior Score (0.0 – 1.0)  
↓  
Behavioral Footprint Tracker  
↓  
Emotional Circuit Breaker  
↓  
Guardian Mode  
↓  
Claw Agents (Risk / Education)  
↓  
User Awareness Feedback

## Core Modules

### Behavioral Engine
Analyzes behavioral signals such as:
- recent losses
- trade frequency
- leverage escalation
- position size changes

### Behavioral Footprint Tracker
Identifies patterns in trading behavior.

### Emotional Circuit Breaker
Evaluates the behavioral risk level.

### Guardian Mode
Provides protective feedback during high-risk moments.

### Claw Agents
Trigger warnings or educational prompts.

## Design Philosophy

HachiClaw focuses on **behavioral awareness**, not market prediction.

The goal is to help traders recognize risky emotional patterns before they lead to losses.
